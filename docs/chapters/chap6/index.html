Chapter 6

Relations, Networks, Flows

In this chapter the focus is squarely on networks (which mathematicians insist of calling graphs) and their potential for more relational understandings of space in giscience. As much of the code associated with the figures in this chapter shows, handling such structure elegantly is not something existing geospatial tools do well.
